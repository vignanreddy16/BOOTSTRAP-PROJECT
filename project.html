Our History



You’ll find the roots of Rolls-Royce way back in January 1904. Two independent car manufacturers named Henry Royce and Charles Rolls made an alliance after admiring each other’s work in the emerging industry. They worked together to produce four models ranging from a £395 ($494) two cylinder 10HP to a £890 ($1114) six cylinder.Those models were unveiled at the Paris Salon in December the same year. They proved an immense success, and the Rolls-Royce Limited brand was formed just over a year later in 1906. Rolls-Royce has been synonymous with luxury and performance for over a century.
